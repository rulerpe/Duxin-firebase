FAQs
How do I scan a document to translate?
Simply open the Duxin app and tap the "Take a picture" button. Position the document you want to translate inside the viewfinder and capture the document, the app will automatically process the image.
What languages does Duxin support?
Duxin currently supports translation between "English", "Chinese", "Spanish", "French", "Korean", "Russian", "Vietnamese", "Filipino".
How do I view previous translations?
Tap the "Account" button on the top right, login to see a list of your past translated documents. You can view or delete them from here.
